# WasteAI User Manual

Welcome to the WasteAI User Manual. This document provides straightforward operating instructions to test, deploy, and leverage the Proof-of-Concept (PoC) waste segregation model.

## 📱 How to Run the App

### Initial Navigation
1. Open the designated distribution URL (`https://...`) on your smart device's preferred web browser (Google Chrome or Safari recommended).
2. If encountering a local development security layout ("Your Connection is Not Private"), click **Advanced** followed by **Proceed / Accept Risk** to clear self-signed SSL blocks.

### Camera Access Authorization
* Upon complete application load, your browser will prompt for camera resource initialization.
* Tap **Allow** or **Grant Camera Permission** to activate the environmental scanning lens.

## ♻️ Executing a Material Scan
1. **Frame the Target:** Point your phone's rear camera lens directly at any everyday discard item (such as an empty plastic bottle, electronic accessory, card packaging, or vegetable scrap).
2. **Scan Execution:** Tap the round **Camera icon** at the bottom center of your browser interface.
3. **Processing State:** The viewport screen will show a pulsing horizontal line overlay and an "Analyzing via Gemini..." loader indicator. Do not close your browser tab during this window.
4. **Result Sheet Navigation:** Within seconds, a native overlay card will glide up from the bottom boundary displaying:
   * Identified Material Item Name.
   * Color-coded category tag indicating exact sorting bin matchings (*Wet, Dry, Hazardous, E-Waste, Recyclable*).
   * Brief disposal methodology instructions.
   * Practical municipal recycling advice.
   * Relevant GHMC Hyderabad collection specifications.
5. **Resetting:** Tap **Scan Another Item** to wipe cached states, hide information layouts, and instantly resume live camera viewfinders.
